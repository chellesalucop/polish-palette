General Information

PolishPalette is an AI-powered web appointment system built using Django. It centralizes bookings for small-scale and home-based nail service businesses to improve efficiency and customer satisfaction.

Unlike chat-based bookings, PolishPalette offers real-time scheduling that prevents overlapping appointments, reduces delays, and eliminates time miscommunication.

No. PolishPalette is fully web-based and accessible through any browser on a mobile phone, tablet, or computer.
For Clients

Clients can select their preferred date and available time slot. Once booked, the system automatically confirms the appointment.

The system suggests nail designs based on user preferences and the technician’s previous work.

Yes. Clients can browse a technician’s portfolio to review previous nail designs before booking.
For Nail Technicians

PolishPalette centralizes appointments, client histories, and service records into one organized system.

Yes. The system automates booking confirmations and schedule management to reduce manual coordination.
